About MBBS

Metiari Bottola Badminton Society (MBBS) was established on November 2nd, 2025 with a vision to promote badminton as a sport and create a community of passionate players in West Bengal.

Our state-of-the-art facility in Pratapnagar, Metiari is equipped with professional-grade courts and training equipment to help players of all levels improve their game.

Whether you're a beginner looking to learn or an experienced player seeking competitive matches, MBBS provides the perfect environment for badminton enthusiasts.
